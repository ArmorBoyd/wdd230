Membership Level Benefits

Non-Profit Org

  • Educational resources: Gain access to workshops, seminars, and resources tailored to the needs of non-profit organizations, helping you to enhance your operational efficiency and effectiveness.

Bronze Membership

  • Basic networking opportunities: Connect with other Chamber members at networking events, mixers, and business expos to expand your professional network.
  • Member discounts: Access exclusive discounts on Chamber programs, services, and events, helping you save on essential business expenses.

Silver Membership

  • Access to Chamber resources: Tap into a wealth of business resources, including market research reports, business planning tools, and best practice guides to support your growth and success.
  • Networking benefits: Participate in exclusive networking events, roundtable discussions, and industry-specific forums to connect with key influencers and decision-makers.
  • Marketing opportunities: Showcase your products, services, and achievements through sponsored content, social media features, and spotlight articles in Chamber communications.

Gold Membership

  • VIP networking access: Gain access to exclusive VIP networking receptions, executive luncheons, and leadership events, providing unparalleled opportunities to connect with industry leaders and decision-makers.
  • Premier listing in Chamber directory: Enjoy top-tier placement in the Chamber's directory, with enhanced visibility and promotion across Chamber communication channels.
  • Customized marketing support: Receive personalized marketing assistance from the Chamber's marketing team, including tailored promotional campaigns, media outreach, and branding support.
  • Strategic partnership opportunities: Collaborate with the Chamber on strategic initiatives, sponsorships, and community projects to demonstrate your commitment to corporate social responsibility and community development.
